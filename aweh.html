What do we invest in ?

We are Flexible, Investing in control and minority positions control investments.

Control investments

Control positions in Web3/Technology companies in distressed or special situations requiring a buyer that can move quickly – our industry specialisation allows us to do just that. We have a strong preference for brands with in-house developer teams and are based in the UK. We do both SAFT & SAFE deals.

Growth Equity

As flexible non-control investors, we invest in everything from traditional post-revenue growth rounds, to turnarounds & special situations as well as Seed rounds and angel rounds. Our operating background means we do more than write a check – we work with you side-by-side to grow your company sustainably by providing access to our team of Web3 & Technology experts. Our team is fully capable of managing all business functions down to a tactical level; portfolio companies often leverage us as an extension of their team.Minority investment targets can be cash-flow positive or negative and are based in the UK, Europe, USA or South Africa. We have a strong preference for early stage Angel & Seed rounds. We do both SAFE & SAFT deals.
